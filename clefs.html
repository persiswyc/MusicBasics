【Clefs】

Bass Clef

It looks like an ear or a rotated unhappy face.
Low-sounding instruments like the cello and the tuba, also the left hand on the piano, will use the bass clef.

Treble Clef

Viola will use the alto clef most of the time. So, it is also called the viola clef.

Treble Clef

High-sounding instruments like the flute and the trumpet, also the right hand on the piano, will use the treble clef.
